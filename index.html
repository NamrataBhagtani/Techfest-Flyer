About the School Techfest

The state school is organising a School Techfest to educate the students on frontier technologies for sustainable development methods, which will be accompanied by workshops, fun activities, and quizzes.

Automated car demonstration

Highly automated and fully autonomous driving are the future. Experience the fully functional automated car simulation and navigate through a busy environment.

VR gaming experience

Take part in the virtual reality tournament, test your abilities in different VR game disciplines. Take part as a team or play alone, and win prizes.

Assess AI robot

Get your AI robot evaluated for its overall performance and its use case as a robot system to become the best AI robot and win exciting prizes.

Tech Quiz

Put your knowledge about all-things-tech to test with our Tech Quiz. Geek it out with other players. Good luck!

Code Soccer

The simulation-based coding event is back, where you code the match strategy for a team of three robots and show your coding skills.

Fix Me

The robot named Robo-lux is having difficulty putting things in the basket. Help it by debugging its code to complete the task.
